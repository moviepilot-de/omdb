mailinglists @ omdb

The omdb project is maintaining a number of public mailinglists. To participate you need to subscribe to it. The list info-en is the only list that allows posts by non-members. So if you do not want to subscribe, send an email to this list.

info-en

General mailinglist for discussions and suggestions regarding the omdb project.

content-en

Mailinglist for concerning questions and organisation of regular editors on omdb.

tech-en

Technical discussion regarding the software and future enhancements of omdb.

new-movies

Information about newly created movies. This mailinglist is read-only.
